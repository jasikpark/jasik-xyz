Why join G-Power?

G-Power is a Texas based Company, which offers very competitive rates for both industrial/commercial and residential products. G-Power cares for its customers and advises them as to how to efficiently use energy, to reduce their electricity expenditures. (Our team of experts will assist you).

G-Power is locally present in Texas and personally assists its customers in dealing with needs with the distribution companies, such as AEP, Sharyland, Oncor, Centerpoint and others. As opposed to most of our competitors, we also have offices in the Rio Grande Valley, which gives our customers huge advantages. You can count on local customer service (Let us do the work for you). G-Power offers unique products to its industrial/commercial and residential customers like no other Retail Energy Provider.
